About

I am a Climate Scientist working on offshore and coastals risks from coastal flooding and recession driven by sea-level rise, storm surges, waves and tides. I am currenty working at University of Central Florida. My current work involves predicting extreme return period values of storm surges for United States using a Bayesian Hierarchial Model (BHM).

Experience

I have worked at Griffith University, CSIRO (Australia), United States Geological Survey (USGS) and at the Coastal Engineering & Risk Lab of the University of Central Florida. I have worked across different national and international projects within my field of expertise.
